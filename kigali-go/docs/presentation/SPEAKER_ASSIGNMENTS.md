## **EMERGENCY PROTOCOLS**

### If Speaker Drops (Technical Issue):
- **Backup:** Member 6 can cover any section
- **Action:** Skip to next slide, other member summarizes quickly

### If Over Time:
- **Priority Slides:** 2, 3, 5, 8 (must cover)
- **Can Abbreviate:** 7, 9
- **Cut if Necessary:** Parts of slides 6, 10

### If Under Time:
- **Expand:** Slide 5 (more feature details)
- **Add:** Brief live demo
- **Elaborate:** User testimonials

### If Technical Failure:
- **Backup:** All members have presentation on 2 devices
- **Alternative:** Verbal presentation without slides (practiced)
- **Support:** Member 6 troubleshoots while others continue
